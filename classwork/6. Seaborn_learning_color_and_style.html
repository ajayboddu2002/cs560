changing figure style¶

Seaborn has 5 different styles: sns.set_style()

  • darkgrid (default style)
  • whitegrid
  • dark
  • white
  • ticks

Seaborn has 4 different contexts: sns.set_context()

  • paper
  • notebook
  • talk
  • poster

Both paramters can be passed in to sns.set_theme(style,context)

In [5]:
sns.set_style('whitegrid')
sns.countplot(data=tips,x='smoker')

scale and context¶

set_context() overrides default parameters

In [24]:
#using set_context()
sns.set_context('poster') #paper, notebook, talk, poster
sns.countplot(data=tips,x='day')
Out[24]:
<AxesSubplot:xlabel='day', ylabel='count'>
In [23]:
#paper, notebook, talk, poster
sns.set_context('paper',font_scale=2)
sns.countplot(data=tips,x='day')
Out[23]:
<AxesSubplot:xlabel='day', ylabel='count'>

sns.set_theme()¶

you can pass both context and style parameters

In [25]:
#context: paper, notebook, talk, poster
#style: darkgrid, whitegrid, dark, white, ticks
sns.set_theme(context='paper',style='white',font_scale=2)
sns.countplot(data=tips,x='day')
Out[25]:
<AxesSubplot:xlabel='day', ylabel='count'>
